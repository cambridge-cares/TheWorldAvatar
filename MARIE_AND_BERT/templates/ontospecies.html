OntoSpecies

OntoSpecies is an ontology for the semantic representation of chemical species and their properties.


Download:

  • Extended OntoSpecies TBox: OntoSpecies_v2.owl.


Access:

  • Blazegraph workbench: http://www.theworldavatar.com/blazegraph/#query.
  • SPARQL endpoint: http://www.theworldavatar.com/blazegraph/namespace/copy_ontospecies_pubchem.
